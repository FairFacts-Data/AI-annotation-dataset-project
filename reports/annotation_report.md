# Annotation Report – AI Response Evaluation Dataset

## Project Overview

This project involved creating and annotating a dataset of AI-generated responses to evaluate performance across multiple dimensions including accuracy, hallucination, bias, helpfulness, safety, and policy compliance.

## Dataset Summary

The dataset consists of prompts and AI-generated responses across multiple topics including factual questions, privacy, responsible AI, governance, safety, and general knowledge questions.

Each response was evaluated and labeled according to the annotation guidelines.

## Annotation Categories

The following categories were used to evaluate each response:

* Accuracy
* Hallucination
* Bias
* Helpfulness
* Safety
* Policy Violation
* Confidence
* Notes

## Observations

During the annotation process, most responses were accurate and safe. Some responses required careful evaluation for bias, safety considerations, or incomplete information. Refusal responses for unsafe or harmful prompts were evaluated as safe and appropriate.

## Safety and Policy Review

Responses that involved harmful or disallowed activities were reviewed to ensure the AI responded appropriately by refusing or providing safe alternatives.

## Conclusion

This project demonstrates the process of designing an annotation task, creating annotation guidelines, labeling AI-generated responses, defining metadata schema, documenting annotation workflow, and summarizing evaluation results.

This workflow reflects typical processes used in AI evaluation, human data annotation, and machine learning data operations.
